Specification

  • Bluetooth Connectivity: Many modern wireless mice use Bluetooth technology to connect directly to a computer or device. This eliminates the need for a USB receiver, making the mouse more compact and ideal for use with laptops or tablets.
  • 2.4 GHz Wireless Receiver: Alternatively, wireless mice can come with a USB receiver that plugs into a computer's USB port. These mice operate on the 2.4 GHz frequency and offer a stable connection. The receiver typically has a small size and can be stored in the mouse itself for portability.
  • Battery-Powered: Most wireless mice run on batteries, either AA or AAA. Battery life varies, but it typically lasts anywhere from 6 months to a year with regular use, depending on the type of battery and mouse features.
